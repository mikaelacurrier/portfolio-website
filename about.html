My Story:

I grew up in the beautiful state of Colorado and fell in love with all things outdoors. In 2017, while finishing my Psychology degree online, I moved to Atlanta, GA, where I currently reside and continue to find the beauty in the world.

Growing up, I was greatly influenced by my father and older brothers. My father was a senior Java developer for the Department of Defense for 15 years and my brothers have shown talents in software engineering from young ages. Their work has always been interesting to me, but since I wasn't confident in my math skills going into University, I chose to focus on Psychology.

I chose this field for my interests in how the brain works and while I will very likely never work as a lab assistant or social worker, I am very grateful for the skills and knowledge that I learned while pursuing this degree. I believe these are skills that I apply to my daily life and future careers. Since becoming a software engingeer, my passion for learning about human behavior has evolved into computer learning and AI.

After moving to Atlanta, I surrounded myself with those in the tech industry: I worked as a nanny for my cousin who owns a tech start-up. After confessing my interest in the field, he pointed me towards Lambda School and I have since fallen in love with software engineering.
